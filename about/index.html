Who is Jeremy?

I'm an all round web developer from Sydney, Australia with five years industry experience. I've been involved in a wide range of projects with my professional front end and back end skills and always delivered excellent service and high quality web development at minimal expense.

BIOGRAPHY

I was born and grew up in Zhengzhou, Henan province of China. I quickly became interested in computing and the Internet when my parents bought me a "Pentium 166" back in 1997. Soon after, I started learning basic web development knowledge (HTML/CSS/ASP) and ran my personal websites until I graduated from Zhengzhou University. In the mean time, I realised I finally find the career I love so much. In 2001, I began my IT career from mydrivers.com. In order to enlarge my vision and improve my technical level, I continued my study in University of Wollongong, Australia in 2003. Two years later, I received my Master degree in Computer Studies there. After a short break in China I came back to Australia and joined dbNet in October 2006 as software systems/web application developer, where my skills and experience get boosted.

SKILLS

Currently, I am primarily using Perl/Catalyst as well as jQuery to develop highly usable, visually attractive and standard based web applications for clients in my work. During my spare time I also learn and try other web development technologies, especially Ruby/Rails, Python/Django, HTML5 and CSS3, to keep my skills polished. In fact, as a tireless seeker of knowledge, I'm passionate to all creative and innovative IT tech and always sync myself with the fast changing world.
